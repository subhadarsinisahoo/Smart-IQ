Franchisee Model

Your Franchisee Model is designed to accomadate a three tier hierarchy with master, area and unit franchisee.

Master Franchisee :
Terms of Association related to Master Franchisee.3-tier
1 . Non-refundable association fees.
2 . 50 % margin unit franchisee fees.
3 . 50 % on royalty receipts from the unit franchisee.
4 . State level franchisee.
5 . Marketing support from the company.
6 . Term of agreement for three(3) years.

Area Franchisee :
Terms of association related to Area Franchisee.
frach money 1 . Non-refundable assocition fees.
2 . 30 % margin from unit franchisee fees.
3 . 30 % margin on royalty receipts from the unit franchisee.
4 . Area level franchisee.
5 . Marketing support from the company.
6 . Terms of the agreement for 3 years.

Unit Franchisee :
Terms of association related to unit franchisee.
1 . Non-refundable franchisee fees. multi layer of work II
2 . Complete ownership of the unit.
3 . Unit wise executivity.
4 . Complete support and training for teacher.
5 . Marketing and branding support.
6 . Agreement tenure for 10 years.
